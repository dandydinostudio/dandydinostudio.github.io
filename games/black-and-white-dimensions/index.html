Black And White Dimensions

Black And White Dimensions is a simple but engaging game where your spaceship has been caught in a gravitational anomaly, causing it to travel between the Black and White dimensions!

While traveling in high speed, you must tap on the screen to change the spaceship dimension and survive the dimension you find yourself into.

  • Black spaceship survives in White dimension;
  • White spaceship survives in Black dimension;
  • Tap on the screen switches the spaceship dimension;

In this deep space challenge we want to know: How long can you survive?
